On Time User Manual

Introduction

On Time is an application that can help you manage your time efficiently. This app acts as both a schedule planner and time tracker to help you view how much time you have spent working on your task.

With On Time, you will be able to monitor how you spend your time on a day to day basis, while still being able to add events to the calendar. This app will provide a tool for you to improve your time management skills by having a simple way to visualize future plans or how you spent your time in the past in the forms of graphs or statistics.

Getting Started

After installing the app, open the app and create a user account if you do not already have one. Enter your email address and create a password on the Register screen. After creating your account, you will then see the home page.

Using the Calendar

Description:

An in-app calendar that also stores your created tasks.

Instruction:

Tap on the Calendar tab button below to navigate to the Calendar page. In this page, you can view the current date and see a weekly view. You can also create a task on the Calendar page. Tap on any date to see what tasks you have for that day, if any.

Scheduling a Task

Description:

This is the primary feature of the app. You can save and conveniently organize all your tasks within the app.

Instruction:

Tap on the Tasks tab button below to navigate to the Tasks page. Tap on the "Add a Task" button. A window will pop up, where you can enter the name, hours, address, and date for your task. A task element will now appear in the list of tasks as well as in the calendar. To remove a task, tap on the "Remove task" button, then tap on the x beside the task element you wish to remove.

Using the Stopwatch

Description:

This feature allows you to use a conventional stopwatch.

Instruction:

Tap on the Timer tab button below to navigate to the stopwatch. To start the clock, tap on the "Start" button. To pause the clock, tap on the "Stop" button. To reset the clock, tap on the "Reset" button.

View Personal Statistics

Description:

This feature allows you to see the statistical data of how you have spent your time since installing the app. The statistics will show you the total amount of time you have spent doing each task you have created, and how much time you spend on certain apps.

Instruction:

Tap on the Statistics tab button to navigate to the Statistics page. You will be able to see the statistical data for each task.

General Settings

Description:

Configure the On Time app to your preference.

Instruction:

Tap on the Settings tab button to navigate to the Settings page. You can view your username and profile icon. In the list of items, you can visit the link to the developer's GitHub repository. You can also tap on the "Log Out" button to log out of yoru account.
